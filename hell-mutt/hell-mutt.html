Synopsis

Hell Mutt is a slow-burning psychological descent into isolation, guilt, and inherited madness. Trapped in a decaying house with his unhinged mother, a frail young man drifts between memory and delusion, haunted by the echoes of a childhood mistake—the moment he unplugged his dying father's life support. Casper spirals into paranoia and violence as he seeks freedom. When reality blurs with desire and madness, Casper must confront the terrifying cost of his escape.
