Synopsis

In collaboration with Triple S Polaris in Cedar City, my crew and I produced a promotional video set against the backdrop of the Cedar Beach Oasis. This project marked my first experience directing and producing a large-scale production, complete with a full film crew and a fleet of off-road machines and non-film personnel. It was a thrilling challenge filled with firsts, teamwork, and creativity—an unforgettable step forward in my journey as a filmmaker and friend.
