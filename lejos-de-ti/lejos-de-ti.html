Synopsis

Lejos De Ti is a tense, emotional journey about a fractured sibling bond pushed to its breaking point. When Eva’s estranged brother Danny returns with a dark confession, she is forced to confront their shared past and the unbearable weight of his actions. In the film’s haunting climax, Eva faces a devastating choice—one that will not only decide Danny’s fate but forever alter her own path, forcing her to reconcile love, pain, and the cost of freedom.
